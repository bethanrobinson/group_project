About us...

Off My Chest is a website that aims to address body confidence and self-image. By confronting how we treat ourselves- how we view our bodies- we hope to create a more kind and inclusive space, that embraces and celebrates bodies of all kinds. In the media and online the body- the feminine body, in particular- is scrutinised and editted and controlled. This is a filter-free zone, where we can love and take joy in our imperfections, because we all have them and they are all natural.

We were able to create this website using what we learnt during our lessons with Code:First. The group introduces women and non-binary people to coding,teaching them how to make their own website. Find out more about the courses here.
