Here, at Off My Chest, we want to encourage a healthy discussion about body image. It is a place where you can anonymously vent about something- on one condition- you must finish your post with something you LOVE about yourself. We hope that through honesty and communication we can face our insecurities and learn to be kinder to ourselves.
